Chicken Tinola

Description

Tinola is an easy, one-pot soup from the Philippines!

Best served over a bed of freshly cooked white rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (1 1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 3 pounds chiken legs and thighs, rinsed and patted dry
  • 2 (14 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 chayote squash, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 head bok choy, chopped
  • 1 (8 ounce) package fresh spinach, chopped
